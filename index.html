The Automated Engine That Drives Repeat Business

Our powerful software works for you 24/7. We set it up once, and it continuously engages your customers with 7 automated touchpoints designed to keep them coming back.

1. The Bounce Back Offer

Encourages a second visit soon after their first, turning a new customer into a repeat customer.

2. The "We Miss You" Campaign

Automatically reaches out to customers who haven't visited in a while with an incentive to return.

3. Electronic Punch Card

Rewards customers for their loyalty after a set number of visits (e.g., "Your 6th visit is on us!").

4. Birthday Rewards

Sends a special, personalized offer on a customer's birthday, making them feel valued.

5. Anniversary Rewards

Celebrates the anniversary of when a customer first joined your loyalty program.

6. Automated Review Requests

After a second visit, it automatically asks happy, returning customers to leave a positive online review.

7. On-Demand Marketing Blasts

While the system is automated, you always have the power to send instant text blasts about special promotions, events, or important updates directly to all your members.
